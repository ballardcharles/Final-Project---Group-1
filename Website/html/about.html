Behind the Scenes

Our team set out to demonstrate the whole of what we learned at Butler University by analyzing and displaying our findings on the stroke prediction dataset.

We decided to split into smaller task forces in order to tackle multiple problems simultaneously. These teams are displayed below, while more detailed information can be found on our contact page.
